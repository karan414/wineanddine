<h2>Wine and Dine</h2>

<p>In this competitive world, reducing cost and increasing productivity become necessary. Particularly, manpower is more expensive for restaurants. In this situation, an automated system can help to increase productivity. It also helps in increasing the efficiency, decrease processing time and increases the experience quality for customers.</p>  
 
<p>This project focuses on developing a food ordering management, order retrieval system, managing menu and evaluating its effects on efficiency of process and customer satisfaction. It has four core items: requirements analysis, implementation, testing and evaluation. As a starting point, a restaurant is identified based on selection criteria. The requirements are gathered from the partner restaurant and user survey. The evaluation of system shows the effects of introducing the system and customer satisfaction.</p>
